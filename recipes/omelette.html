Omelette

Omelettes are whisked eggs fried in a pan. You can fold them around fillings such as proteins, vegetables, and cheeses

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • Season and Fillings of your preference
  • Oil or butter for frying
  • Hot sauce and green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Begin by whisking your egg(s) in a cup while your pan gets to medium heat. Don't forget to season
  2. Coat your pan in butter or oil and pour in the egg mixture
  3. Once the edges of the eggs begin to set, move the cooked eggs into the middle and allow the raw egg to move to the outsides of the pan
  4. Continue doing this until they are about 80% cooked then throw in your fillings of choice

Although omelettes have very simple ingredients they are truly a dish that must be mastered! Don't give up if it didn't come out as expected. Try again with different ingredients
