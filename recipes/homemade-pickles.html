Homemade Pickles

Pickles made your garden cucumbers are a delicious summer snack

Ingredients

  • Garden cucumbers
  • Vinegar
  • Canning jar
  • Seasonings and flavors of your choice, such as: sweet onions, peppers, dill, garlic, and sweeteners

Instructions

  1. Slice your cucumbers to your desired size and thickness
  2. Whisk together a basic brine made of water, vinegar and seasonings/sweeteners
  3. Pack your cucumbers into a jar, add some dill and garlic, and pour the brine over it all
  4. Refrigerate until the pickles taste sufficiently "pickled!"
