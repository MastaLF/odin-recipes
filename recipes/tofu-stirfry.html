Tofu Stir Fry

Tofu stir fry is a delicious and healthy meal that can be made with many different ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Tofu (extra firm recommended)
  • Sauce and seasonings of your liking
  • Vegetables
  • Noodles

Instructions

  1. Start by cutting the tofu into cubes and prepping your veggies. Marinate the tofu for at least 1 hour
  2. Get your pan heating up nicely while you get everything ready. Cook your noodles in boiling water but make sure to leave them undercooked
  3. Cook your tofu in oil (add corn starch to make them extra crispy!)
  4. Once the tofu is finished cooking throw in your vegetables; followed by the noodles. Add any extra sauce and mix around. Serve hot
